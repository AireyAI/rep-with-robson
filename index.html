FAQ · 05

Questions, answered.

First time seeing Jord? These are the questions most people ask before they book. Anything else, just drop him a message.

Is sports massage only for athletes?

No — sports massage techniques work brilliantly for anyone with tension, pain, postural issues, or who spends long hours at a desk or on their feet. You don't need to lift weights to benefit.

What should I wear?

Come in gym gear — shorts and a t-shirt, or comfortable workout clothes. You'll stay clothed during the session except for the specific area being treated, which will be draped with a towel.

Does it hurt?

It shouldn't be unbearable. There's sometimes a "good pain" when working through tight knots — the kind that feels intense in the moment but brings relief straight after. Jord checks pressure throughout the session. You're in control.

How often should I come?

Most clients book a maintenance session every 4–6 weeks. If you're training for a race, rehabbing an injury, or lifting seriously, weekly or fortnightly sessions work better. Jord will advise after your first session based on what he finds.

Do I need a GP referral?

No — you can book directly. If your issue is medical (recent surgery, active disease, pregnancy, open injury), Jord will ask you to check in with your GP or consultant first before starting treatment.

What's the difference between a Maintenance Massage and an Injury Assessment?

Maintenance Massage is a full-body sports massage focused on keeping you in good condition between training blocks — tension out, mobility in. Injury Assessment & Treatment includes a clinical evaluation of a specific problem area, hands-on treatment (dry needling or cupping where it helps), and a home exercise plan to follow.

Will cupping leave marks?

Dry cupping can leave circular pink or red marks that usually fade within 3–7 days. They're bruises from the suction — not damage — and they're a sign blood flow is being drawn into tight tissue. If you have an event where you don't want visible marks, let Jord know and he'll adjust the treatment.

How do I pay?

Card, bank transfer, or cash — payment is taken after your session. Block bookings and gift vouchers coming soon.

What's the cancellation policy?

Free to cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your slot. Cancellations within 24 hours are charged 50% of the session fee. No-shows are charged the full fee — this helps keep the calendar fair for other clients. Full details on the terms page.

Can I book for someone else?

Absolutely — just let Jord know at booking that the session is for someone else, and pass on their details. Gift vouchers coming soon.
