Privacy
Policy.

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains what personal information REP with Robson (Jordan Robson, trading as a sole trader) collects from you, how it's used, and your rights under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact Jord at jordanrobson94@outlook.com or 07376 369795.

1. Who runs this site

REP with Robson is operated by Jordan Robson, a sole-trader sports therapist based above Blackfriars Tattoo, 1 Lonsdale Street, Carlisle CA1 1BJ.

2. What we collect

When you book a session

  • Your full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Chosen treatment, date and time
  • Any notes you add about your injury, goals or medical history

When you leave a review

  • Your name (first name is shown publicly once approved)
  • Email address (used for moderation contact only; never shown publicly)
  • Your review text and star rating

When you visit the site

If you accept the cookie banner, a single Google Analytics 4 (GA4) cookie records anonymous usage data — pages viewed, device type, country, referring site. IP addresses are anonymised before storage. Declining the banner means no analytics cookies are set at all.

3. Why we use it

  • Deliver your treatment. Booking details are used to arrange your session and send reminders, under the lawful basis of contract performance.
  • Clinical safety. Notes about injuries or medical history help Jord tailor treatment safely — legitimate interest and, where relevant, explicit consent.
  • Publish reviews. Your review is shown (first name only) to help other potential clients — legitimate interest; you may withdraw at any time.
  • Improve the site. Anonymised analytics help identify which pages are useful — consent only, never without your opt-in.

4. Who we share it with

We don't sell or rent your data. It is shared only with:

  • Google Calendar — to add your booking to Jord's calendar and send you a confirmation invite.
  • Email service (Outlook / SMTP) — to send booking confirmations and reminders.
  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymised site usage only, and only with your consent.
  • Hosting (GitHub Pages + Railway) — standard web hosting.

All processors are contractually required to keep your data secure and not use it for their own purposes.

5. How long we keep it

  • Booking records and treatment notes: 7 years (professional clinical retention requirement for insurance).
  • Published reviews: indefinitely, unless you ask us to remove them.
  • Rejected reviews and pending submissions: deleted after 30 days.
  • Analytics data: aggregated, retained for 14 months.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Ask for a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct anything that's inaccurate
  • Ask us to delete your data (except where we must retain it for legal or insurance reasons)
  • Object to, or restrict, our processing
  • Ask us to port your data to another provider
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights email Jord at jordanrobson94@outlook.com. Most requests are actioned within a week, and always within the 30-day legal limit.

7. Cookies

The site uses one type of cookie:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymised site usage tracking. Only loaded if you click "Accept" on the cookie banner.

To review or change your choice, click Cookie Settings.

8. Security

Booking and review data is transmitted over HTTPS. Treatment notes are stored in password-protected systems accessible only to Jord. Payment details are not stored at all — payments are taken in person at the end of your session.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top shows when. Material changes will be announced on the homepage.
